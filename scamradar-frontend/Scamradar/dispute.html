Is your number falsely reported?

ScamRadar only shows community-reported data, not verified facts. If you believe a report about you is false, malicious, or made in error — you have the right to dispute it. We take every dispute seriously.

⚖️ Submit a Dispute

Fill in the details below. Our grievance team will review within 72 hours as per IT Act 2000 intermediary guidelines.

📋 IT Act Section 79 — Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act 2000, ScamRadar is an intermediary platform displaying user-generated content. You have the right to request removal of content about you. We are required to acknowledge your complaint within 24 hours and resolve it within 15 days.

🛡️ Our Safety Policies

  • Entities need 3+ independent reports to appear publicly
  • All results carry a "community-reported, not verified" disclaimer
  • Risk scores decay over time if no new reports come in
  • Disputed entities are marked clearly in search results
  • Single-report entities are never shown publicly
  • We never say someone "is a scammer" — only that they were reported
